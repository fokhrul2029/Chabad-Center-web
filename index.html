Everybody Come Get Chai!

High Holiday Services at
The Chelsea Chabad Family Shul in NYC

This year, step into the New Year with meaning, music, and community at the Chelsea Chabad Family Shul. Whether you’re Reform, Conservative, Orthodox, or completely unaffiliated — at Chabad, we see you simply as Jewish.

Our services are soulful yet easy to follow, with song, storytelling, and English-Hebrew prayer books to keep everyone engaged. Kids enjoy fun and interactive youth programming designed just for them, while adults are inspired by uplifting messages and melodies.

Come celebrate the start of 5786 with a community that feels like family.

Services will take place at
The Family Shul.
